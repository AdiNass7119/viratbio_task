about

Virat Kohli – The Chase Master Virat Kohli, one of India's finest cricketers, was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi. Known for his passion, aggression, and consistency, he has become a global cricketing icon. Kohli made his international debut in 2008, and quickly rose through the ranks with his talent. He is especially known for his chasing abilities, earning the nickname “The Chase Master.” As the captain of India, he led with heart and intensity, taking India to new heights across all formats. He has scored over 70 international centuries, making him one of the highest century-makers ever. Off the field, Kohli is admired for his discipline, fitness, and commitment to the game. He married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in 2017, and they are one of India's most loved celebrity couples. Kohli’s journey is marked by hard work and determination, inspiring millions of young athletes worldwide. With a fierce drive and never-give-up attitude, Virat Kohli remains a true legend of the sport.

Achievements

Most centuries in ODI cricket – 50 centuries (Surpassing Sachin Tendulkar in 2023). ICC Cricketer of the Decade – Awarded in 2020 for dominance from 2011–2020. Fastest to 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, 11,000 & 12,000 ODI runs. 100+ international centuries – Among the few in cricket history. T20I milestones – First player to score 4,000 runs in T20 Internationals. Most Player of the Match awards in T20 World Cups. Led India to historic Test series win in Australia (2018–19). Only Indian captain to win 40+ Test matches. Most double centuries by an Indian in Tests as captain. Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award (2018) – India's highest sporting honor. Padma Shri (2017) – One of India’s most prestigious civilian awards. Wisden Leading Cricketer in the World – Three times (2016, 2017, 2018). Highest run-scorer in IPL history – Over 7,000 runs for Royal Challengers Bangalore. Most successful Indian Test captain – 68 matches, 40 wins. Held No.1 ICC ranking in all formats at different points in his career.

Educational Life

Virat Kohli studied at Vishal Bharti Public School in Delhi, where he developed a strong interest in cricket. Later, he joined the West Delhi Cricket Academy at the age of 9, under coach Rajkumar Sharma. His schooling continued alongside rigorous cricket training and matches. Due to his early cricket commitments, he could not pursue higher education. Despite that, his discipline and dedication shaped him into a world-class athlete.

Marriage

Virat Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11, 2017. The wedding was a private ceremony held in Tuscany, Italy, attended by close family and friends. It was a perfect blend of elegance and tradition, celebrated widely across India. They are considered one of the most admired celebrity couples in the country. In January 2021, they welcomed their daughter Vamika into the world.
